constellation
- A group of stars forming a recognizable pattern in the sky.
    - Example: the constellation Orion 猎户座
- A group of related people or things.
    - Example: a constellation of talents 一众人才

downfall
the loss of power, success or reputation; a sudden fall.
**Examples**:
- Greed led to his downfall. 贪婪致使他身败名裂。
- the downfall of an empire 帝国的覆灭

anguish
**n.** extreme mental or physical pain and suffering
**v.** feel or cause severe pain
**Examples**
- emotional anguish 精神痛苦
- She cried out in anguish. 她痛苦地呼喊。

have sb figured out
to understand someone's character, thoughts or behaviour completely
**Examples**
1. I've finally got you figured out. 我总算看透你了。
2. Don't think you have me figured out. 别以为你很了解我。

belligerent
**adj.**
Definition: aggressive and eager to fight or argue; hostile
**Examples**
- a belligerent attitude 咄咄逼人的态度
- belligerent behaviour 寻衅滋事的行为
**n.**
a country or group engaged in war 交战方
