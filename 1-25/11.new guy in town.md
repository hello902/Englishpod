## 4. gossip

- dish the dirt (informal)
- chatter
- rumour-monger

#### Nouns

- gossipmonger (someone who gossips a lot)
- rumor / rumour
- hearsay
- chitchat
- small talk
- tea (slang: “What’s the tea?” = What’s the gossip?)
