# 1. in a bit of a hurry / in a rush

（有点赶时间 / 匆匆忙忙）

### 同义短语

- be in a hurry
- be pressed for time
- be short on time
- be rushed off one’s feet（特别忙、忙到脚不沾地）
- be in a tearing hurry（非常急）

### 例句

- I’m **in a bit of a hurry**. Can we talk later?
- Sorry, I’m **in a rush**.

---

# 2. slip one’s mind

（被我忘了、一下子想不起来）

### 同义短语

- escape one’s memory
- go right out of my head
- completely forget

### 例句

- Your name **slipped my mind**.
- It completely **slipped my mind** that we had a meeting.

---

# 3. around the corner

（即将来临、就在附近）

### 同义短语

- coming soon
- just ahead
- near at hand
- on the way

### 例句

- Exams are **around the corner**.
- There’s a café **around the corner**.

---

# 4. forgetful

（健忘的）

### 同义词

- absent-minded（心不在焉的）
- memory-impaired（偏正式）
- have a bad memory

### 搭配

- be **forgetful** of details
- become more **forgetful** with age

---

# 5. acquaintance

（熟人、认识的人）

### 相关词

- **a nodding acquaintance** 点头之交
- **a close friend** 亲密朋友
- **a colleague** 同事
- **a stranger** 陌生人

### 例句

- He’s just an **acquaintance**, not a friend.
- I have many **acquaintances** but few close friends.
