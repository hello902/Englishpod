# 1. bridesmaid

- Meaning: a female friend or relative who helps the bride on her wedding day
- Related words:
    
    - **maid of honor** (chief bridesmaid)
    - **bridesmaids’ dresses**
    - **bridesmaid bouquet**
    - **matron of honor** (married maid of honor)
    

# 2. ring bearer

_(You probably meant **ring bearer**, not bring bearer)_

- Meaning: a young boy who carries the wedding rings
- Related:
    
    - **ring pillow**
    - **flower girl** (girl who scatters flowers)
    - **page boy**
    

# 3. nephew & niece

- nephew: son of your brother/sister
- niece: daughter of your brother/sister
- Family extensions:
    
    - **cousin**
    - **uncle / aunt**
    - **grandnephew / grandniece**
    - **in-laws**: brother-in-law, sister-in-law
    

# 4. priest

- Meaning: a religious leader who performs the wedding ceremony
- Related roles:
    
    - **minister**
    - **pastor**
    - **rabbi** (Jewish)
    - **imam** (Muslim)
    - **officiant** (general word for wedding leader)
    - **celebrant**
    

# 5. bride & groom

- bride: woman getting married
- groom: man getting married
- Related:
    
    - **newlyweds** (just married couple)
    - **fiancé** (engaged man)
    - **fiancée** (engaged woman)
    - **betrothed**
    - **spouse** (husband/wife)
    

# 6. aisle

- Meaning: the path down which the bride walks
- Collocations:
    
    - **walk down the aisle**
    - **center aisle**
    - **aisle seat**
    - **church aisle**
